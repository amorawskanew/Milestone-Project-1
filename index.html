Who we are

Anna Morawska


We are a group of passionate and committed filmmakers who work together to create visual art…and we make magic happen.

We take you to places you’ve never been to and inside the skin of people you’ve never met. We offer you a glimpse into the world of fantasy and a thrill of adventure.

In our films anything and everything is possible. We hold your hand as we walk you through the unknown and mysterious worlds. We open your eyes to new wonders. Come with us..It’s just the begining…Everything you want is on the other side of fear.


Our mission is to create visual art that makes people dream.
